**Adaptability** is the capacity to adjust to new conditions, learn from experience, and thrive in changing environments.

## Key Aspects

- **Flexibility**: Willingness to change approach
- **Learning**: Ability to acquire new skills
- **Openness**: Receptivity to new ideas
- **Pragmatism**: Adjusting methods to fit reality

## Why It Matters

- Enables survival and success in changing conditions
- Prevents obsolescence and stagnation
- Allows for innovation and improvement
- Essential for growth and development
