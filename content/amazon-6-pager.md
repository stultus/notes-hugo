**Amazon's 6-Pager** is a narrative-driven decision document format used at Amazon for meetings and strategic proposals.

## Key Characteristics

- Written in prose, not bullet points
- Maximum of 6 pages
- Read silently at the beginning of meetings
- Forces clear, complete thinking
- Narrative structure tells a story

## Purpose

The 6-pager format ensures:
- Rigorous thinking before meetings
- Equal information access for all participants
- Focus on substance over presentation skills
- Better decision-making through written clarity
