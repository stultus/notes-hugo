The **Genomic Sequencing Service** is an NHS service that can sequence your genome to early detect health conditions due to changes to a few of your genes.

## Service Information

- **Service**: [NHS Genomic Medicine Service](https://www.england.nhs.uk/genomics/nhs-genomic-med-service/)
- **Purpose**: Early detection of health conditions through genomic sequencing
