Research indicates a divergence in ROI between influencer types:

* **Nano Influencers (<10k followers)**: 
    - High engagement and authenticity.
    - Average return: ~$1,000 on a $50 investment (20x ROI).
    - Perceived as "just like us," leading to higher trust.

* **Macro Influencers (>100k followers)**:
    - High reach but lower relative engagement.
    - Average return: ~$6,000 on a >$1,000 investment (6x ROI).

On a cost basis, nano influencers consistently outperform macro influencers in engagement and revenue generation.
