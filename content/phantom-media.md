**Phantom Media** is a concept analogous to [Phantom Urbanism](phantom-urbanism) - media properties that exist primarily as financial instruments rather than as actual content or news sources.

## Characteristics

- Media properties devoid of actual news or content
- Designed as investment vehicles rather than information sources
- Ownership by investment firms, focus on metrics over quality
- Content farms, SEO-driven articles, AI-generated content at scale
