A **Polymath** is a person who has comprehensive knowledge or learning in multiple fields; a universal man or woman.

- Literal translation from Greek: "Having learned much".

[Robert Root-Bernstein](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Root-Bernstein), a researcher on polymathy, contrasts them with:
- **Specialist**: Deep knowledge in one area.
- **Dilettante**: Superficial knowledge in many areas.
