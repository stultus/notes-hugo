**Process Documentation** is the practice of recording and tracking your work as you go, becoming a documentarian of what you do.

## Core Idea

This isn't about making art—it's about simply keeping track of what's going on around you.

## Benefits

- See your work more clearly
- Feel like you're making progress
- Build a surplus of material to share
- Create a record of your journey

## What to Document

- Research
- References
- Drawings
- Plans
- Sketches
- Interviews
- Prototypes
- Demos
- Inspiration
- Stories

## How to Document

- Audio recordings
- Photographs
- Video
- Pinboards
- Journals
- Drafts
- Diagrams
- Notes
- Scrapbooks
- Collections

## Practices

- Start a work journal
- Write thoughts in a notebook or speak into an audio recorder
- Keep a scrapbook
- Take photographs of work at different stages
- Shoot video of yourself working
