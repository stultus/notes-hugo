A **Prototype** is an early sample, model, or release of a product built to test a concept or process.

Related to [Proof of Concept (POC)](proof-of-concept) and [Minimum Viable Product (MVP)](minimum-viable-product). Often discussed in [Engineering Strategy](engineering-strategy).
