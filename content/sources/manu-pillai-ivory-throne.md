# The Ivory Throne by Manu S Pillai

## About the Book

**The Ivory Throne: Chronicles of the House of Travancore** is a historical work by Manu S Pillai that chronicles the history of the Travancore royal family, with particular focus on Sethu Lakshmi Bayi, the last Maharani Regent of Travancore.

## Key Topics Covered

- The history of the Travancore royal family
- The life and reign of Sethu Lakshmi Bayi
- Colonial interactions and political dynamics
- Social and cultural history of Kerala
- The legend of Prester John (mentioned in the context of European exploration and encounters with India)
- Chinese settlement in Calicut and the Chinna Kribala community
