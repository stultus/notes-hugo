## Meaning
**'Stultus'** is a Latin word meaning 'Stupid' or 'Foolish'. 

On the internet, "stultus" is the pseudonym adopted by [Hrishikesh Bhaskaran](https://stultus.in/about).

## Usage
I use `stultus` as a handle on various platforms. Variations include `stultusz`, `stultuzz`, or `_stultus`.
