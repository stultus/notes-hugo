## Meaning
**stultus** is a Latin word meaning "stupid" or "foolish".

On the internet, **stultus** is the pseudonym adopted by [Hrishikesh Bhaskaran](https://stultus.in/about/) — a software engineer, FOSS contributor, and screenwriter based in Kerala, India. The handle also appears as the root of the domain [stultus.in](https://stultus.in/).

## Why stultus?

The name is a nod to a Socratic idea: acknowledging what you don't know is the first step to learning. Calling yourself stupid keeps the ego in check. It's also just a good-sounding word.

> "Genius has limits. Stupidity doesn't." — often attributed to Einstein

## Usage

I use `stultus` as a handle across the internet. Common variations:

- `stultus` — the canonical form
- `stultusz`
- `stultuzz`
- `_stultus`

You'll find me as **stultus** on GitHub, as **_stultus** on X (Twitter), as **stultusz** on Instagram, and as **stultus.hrishi** on Facebook.
